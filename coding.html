Coding

Marketing is changing quickly and being able to rustle up a nice press release or team blog is no longer enough. You need to build, automate, and design - or at least know how to use AI to do so.

This page is where I'll share links to the various little projects I'm working on. I'll also crowbar in some stupid stuff that I come across as I figure out how it all works.

First internal tool

Media Scraper

Media Scraper was the first internal tool I built. It's a Python script for making your own curated news roundup: it scrapes a list of websites, filters recent articles, ranks them, groups them by publication, and posts the result to Slack. Here's the repo if you'd like to download it and customise it to your own interests.
